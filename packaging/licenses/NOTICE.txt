Live Captions — Third-Party Notices
===================================

Live Captions bundles third-party software. This notice lists the components
whose licenses impose obligations on redistribution, and how this project meets
them. Commercial distribution of the bundled installer is permitted.

Where a license requires its full text to accompany the binary, that text is
shipped alongside this file in packaging/licenses/ (see "Full-text files" at the
end). Model WEIGHTS are downloaded on first run, not bundled — their licenses
are covered separately in the app documentation.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LGPL components (must remain replaceable + ship license text)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PySide6 / shiboken6 — LGPL v3
    Qt for Python. Bundled as loose, replaceable DLLs in the --onedir layout,
    satisfying the LGPL relinking requirement (the user may substitute their own
    build of the Qt libraries). Full text: LICENSE.LGPLv3.txt.
    Source: https://download.qt.io/official_releases/QtForPython/

soxr (python-soxr / libsoxr) — LGPL v2.1
    Audio resampling. The one easy-to-miss LGPL obligation in this stack. Loose,
    replaceable native library. Full text: LICENSE.LGPLv2.1.txt.
    Source: https://github.com/dofuuz/python-soxr  and  https://sourceforge.net/projects/soxr/

nvidia-cublas-cu12 (cublas64_12.dll, cublasLt64_12.dll)
    Redistributable under the NVIDIA CUDA Toolkit EULA (the wheel's Attachment A
    redistribution terms). The NVIDIA license text is shipped verbatim as
    CUDA-License.txt.
